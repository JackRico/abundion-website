Founded in 2026

We were founded in sunny Southern California by a small group of family members with deep affinity for trading and technology. Our team is nimble, yet we trade a range of asset classes. We innovate in tech and write and use our own software. We approach the uncertainty of markets with quantitative thinking. We dig deep to understand edge cases and tail risks that matter when markets are volatile. We always seek out and partner with other A-players. This keeps our trading sharp and allows us to provide liquidity even during times when the market defies expectations. We start from first principles and use science to solve hairy engineering problems. Our work-hard-play-hard ethic helps us form tight bonds.

Our Core Values

Ethics and Honesty

We take pride that our trading doesn't impose hidden costs or counterparty risk on the market. No one at ABUNDION will be asked to defend a position or state an opinion that they don't believe in. It is important to us that interactions with us or any member of our team are conducted honestly and with integrity.

Risk Management

We take a risk-first approach. We analyze how trading risks might shape out to be bigger or more interconnected than is obvious. This allows us to act boldly and secure our profitable growth without betting the proverbial farm. We don't take excessive risks, and we don't ask others to bail us out. When we make mistakes, we openly cherish the opportunity to learn, with no finger-pointing.

Humility and Curiosity

We remain open-minded and want to humbly learn from other innovators. Frequently, our path leads us far beyond understanding what time it is. We want to understand how the clock works.

Flat Hierarchies

Red tape does nothing for us and seniority isn't everything. Our open-door policy makes it easy for everyone to connect.
